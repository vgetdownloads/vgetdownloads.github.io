We use Google Analytics for Firebase, a third-party service, to collect this data. This service may collect information such as:

This data is aggregated and anonymized and does not personally identify you. We use this information solely for the purpose of improving the app's performance, user experience, and features. For more information on the privacy practices of Google, please visit the Google Privacy & Terms web page.
